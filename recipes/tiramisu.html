Tiramisu

.

Description

.

Tiramisu is an Italian dessert made of ladyfinger pastries dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar and mascarpone and flavoured with cocoa.

Ingredients

.
  • Coffee
  • Ladyfinger Cookies
  • Mascarpone
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Sugar

Instructions

.
  1. Soak your ladyfinger cookies in coffee.
  2. In a large bowl, mix and then whip together your mascarpone, eggs, and sugar until combined and fluffy.
  3. Assemble the tiramisu by laying out alternating layers of coffee soaked cookies and your mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dust the top of the tiramisu with cocoa powder until completely covered.
  5. Let chill overnight.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
