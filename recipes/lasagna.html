Odin Recipes

Description

Lasagna, also known as lasagne, is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made in very wide, flat sheets. The same-named Italian dish is made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Oregano
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese

Instructions

  1. Parboil your lasagna sheets to prepare for baking.
  2. While the sheets are cooking, cook your ground beef in a medium saucepan.
  3. Add your marinara sauce to the pan with the ground beef and let simmer.
  4. Assemble your lasagna by laying some sauce into a baking pan, adding a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by a layer or ricotta cheese. Repeat until the pan is full.
  5. Top the layers with slices of fresh mozarella and oregano.
  6. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes, for the last 5 minutes set the oven to broil.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
