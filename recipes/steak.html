Odin Recipes

Description

A steak is a thick cut of meat generally sliced across the muscle fibers, sometimes including a bone. It is normally grilled or fried. Steak can be diced, cooked in sauce, such as in steak and kidney pie, or minced and formed into patties, such as hamburgers.

Ingredients

  • 1-inch thick Ribeye
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Thyme
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Heat some oil in a cast iron skillet until it is ripping hot.
  2. Add your ribeye to the pan and sear on both sides until golden brown.
  3. Once both sides have been seared, add a stick of butter and your garlic and thyme to the pan and baste the steak with it until it reaches the desired internal temperature.
  4. Remove the steak from the pan, and let rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
